Ron's Campus Parking Services

A Service to find Parking Space at CSUSB

Why?

Have you ever been frustrated circling around campus looking for available parking, on the verge of running late to your class? Do you wish there was an easy way to circumvent the twenty, thirty minutes, maybe hour on the worst days? Do you wish, at a glance, you could pull up on your phone which parking slots were free, and which were taken?

Well, wish no longer! Today, Ron's Campus Parking Services has got you covered!

About

This service is created to be used by CSUSB students. This service was also created for the 2020 AWS Hackthon.
